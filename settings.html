About This Project

BATRSACO is a capstone-level fleet monitoring system designed to provide real-time GPS tracking, 4G LTE connectivity, and event-based video capture for commercial vehicles operating in Batangas province. Built around the ESP32 platform, the system aims to improve driver safety, fleet accountability, and operational transparency.
